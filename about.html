My Story

I’m a UX designer with a background in architectural design. Previously an architect and project manager, I specialized in researching and programming for commercial projects, and providing design solutions that balanced client’s vision, technical limitations and end users experience. I successfully delivered building designs that occupants enjoy living and working in, from conceptual ideas to final completion.

After more than 15 years in this field, I wanted to apply my design skills to a digital world that has more influence on people’s life than ever before. I thoroughly enjoy incorporating both my design specialty and project management experience to bring satisfying digital products to users.
